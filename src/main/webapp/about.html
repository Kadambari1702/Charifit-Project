Who We Are

Charifit was founded with the conviction that no individual should lack access to basic necessities—safe drinking water, nourishment, healthcare, and baseline literacy.

Our platform serves as a vital bridge connecting kind-hearted donors globally with verified community programs. Every dollar is tracked directly from receipt through deployment. We run direct operations with fully local partners on the ground, bypassing heavy intermediary administration fees so that the maximum percentage of donations directly benefits children and families in need.
